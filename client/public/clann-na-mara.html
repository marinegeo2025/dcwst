Clann na Mara

Clann na Mara is a Gaelic surf and ocean culture club for children in Eilean Siar. Through surf games, nature learning, and play, we help kids build confidence, learn water safety, and form a lifelong connection to the sea. All activities are bilingual and rooted in community values, promoting a love for nature and the protection of our coastline. To be a part of Clann na Mara you don’t have to be born here, but you better respect the ocean and its people here.

Clann na Mara. People of the Ocean. Rooted at the edge, where land meets ocean. Living with storms, dancing with tides. Treun hearts. Dòchas unbroken.

From crossings come kinship. From hardship, strength. This is not a story of loss... But of fortitude, renewal, and belonging.

We are not displaced, we belong. Not dispossessed, we are happy. Not destitute, rich in spirit. The land bears memories of our sweat. The ocean flows in our hearts.

This grassroots initiative is designed to connect young children — especially those in remote Gaelic speaking communities — with the natural marine environment through engaging, fun, and safe beach-based activities.

We aim to build ocean awareness and resilience, social and environmental responsibility, and personal wellbeing by introducing children (ages ~6) to the joys of ocean play, surf safety, and coastal morphology. Through a 3-day nature-based program of surf games, beach challenges, creative learning, competition, and storytelling in both Gaelic and English, children will develop physical confidence, mental resilience, and increase their bond with the ocean.

We are planning a GROM SQUAD SURF CLUB with a 3-DAY SCHEDULE over the summer, with potential to grow and develop

We would like to be able to have a small team to run the event and to be able to acquire equipment suitable for young children, including foam boards, balance tools, lifejackets, and wetsuits. Modern wetsuits are extremely flexible and light and insulate the wearer from the elements, meaning that beach activities could be conducted in all weather conditions.

The club will be delivered in close collaboration with Spòrsnis in Ness and Stornoway Karate Club.

By delivering the project entirely within the Outer Hebrides using Gaelic, we will also celebrate and strengthen Gaelic cultural identity through the language of the sea. Clann na Mara is a safe, positive space where children and families connect to nature, community, and heritage.

The long-term vision is to run seasonal versions of this club: 1) helping build a generation of young people empowered to manage and enjoy their ocean resources, 2) developing confident, capable ocean people, and 3) strengthening Gaelic stewardship of the island’s world-class wave resources.

Clann na Mara

Clann na Mara ’na chlub surf is cultar mara Ghàidhealach do chlann ann an Eilean Siar. Tron gheamannan surf, ionnsachadh mu nàdar, agus cluich, bidh sinn a’ cuideachadh chloinne gus misneachd a thogail, ionnsachadh mu sàbhailteachd na mara, agus ceangal fad-beatha a dhèanamh ris a’ chuan. Tha a h-uile gnìomh dà-chànanach agus stèidhichte air luachan coimhearsnachd, a’ brosnachadh gaol don nàdar agus dìon oirthir na h-Alba.

Cha leig thu leas a bhith air do bhreith an seo gus a bhith nar buill – ach feumaidh tu spèis a thoirt don chuan agus do dhaoine an àite.

Tha an iomairt seo, a tha stèidhichte sa choimhearsnachd, ag amas air clann òga – gu sònraichte feadhainn ann an sgìrean iomallach far a bheil Gàidhlig fhathast làidir – a cheangal ri àrainneachd mhara nàdarra tron spòrs, sàbhailteachd agus ionnsachadh cruthachail air a’ tràigh.

Tha sinn airson mothachadh air a’ chuan, uallach sòisealta is àrainneachdail, agus sunnd pearsanta a neartachadh le bhith a’ toirt chothroman do chlann (timcheall air 6 bliadhna a dh'aois) cluiche agus ionnsachadh mu dheidhinn sàbhailteachd is morf-eòlas na tràghad. Tron phrògram trì-latha seo, gheibh iad misneachd corporra, seasmhachd inntinn, agus ceangal nas doimhne ris a’ chuan.

Tha sinn an dùil prògram 3-latha a ruith as t-samhradh leis a’ GROM SQUAD SURF CLUB – le sealladh gum fàs is gun leasaich e thar ùine.

Bu toigh leinn sgioba bheag fhastadh gus an tachartas a ruith, agus uidheamachd a cheannach a tha freagarrach do chlann bheaga: bòrdan foam, innealan cothromachaidh, seacaidean-teasairginn, agus wetsuits. Tha wetsuits ùr-nodha gu math aotrom is sùbailte, agus a’ toirt dìon anns a h-uile aimsir.

Thèid a’ chlub a lìbhrigeadh ann an co-obrachadh dlùth le Spòrsnis ann an Nis agus Club Karate Steòrnabhagh.

Le bhith a’ lìbhrigeadh a’ phròiseict gu tur taobh a-staigh nan Eilean Siar agus sa Ghàidhlig, brosnaichear is neartaichear dearbh-aithne Ghàidhealach tron chànan is cultar a’ chuain.

Is e an lèirsinn fhada a bhith a’ ruith chlubaichean ràitheil gus: 1) ginealach de dhaoine òga a thogail a tha misneachail anns a’ chuan agus eòlach air goireasan na mara aca fhèin, 2) daoine comasach sa mhuir a leasachadh, agus 3) àrdachadh a thoirt air uallach Gàidhealach airson stòrasan tonnan aig ìre cruinne nan eilean.
