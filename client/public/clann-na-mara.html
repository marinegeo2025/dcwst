Clann na Mara

Clann na Mara is a Gaelic surf and ocean culture club for children in Eilean Siar. Through surf games, nature learning, and play, we help kids build confidence, learn water safety, and form a lifelong connection to the sea. All activities are bilingual and rooted in community values, promoting a love for nature and the protection of our coastline. We aim to build ocean awareness and resilience, social and environmental responsibility, and personal wellbeing by introducing children (ages ~6) to the joys of ocean play, surf safety, and coastal morphology. Through a 3-day nature-based program of surf games, beach challenges, creative learning, competition, and storytelling in both Gaelic and English, children will develop physical confidence, mental resilience, and increase their bond with the ocean.
