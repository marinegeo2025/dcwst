Clann na Mara

Clann na Mara is a Gaelic surf and ocean culture club for children in Eilean Siar. Through surf games, nature learning, and play, we help kids build confidence, learn water safety, and form a lifelong connection to the sea. All activities are bilingual and rooted in community values, promoting a love for nature and the protection of our coastline.
